# Settlement Estimate Data Reference

> **Data Sources:** Insurance industry reports (Insurance Information Institute, National Safety Council), Thomson Reuters case data, and major law firm published settlement data from 2019-2024.

## 1. Motor Vehicle Accidents

| Severity | Range | Notes |
|----------|-------|-------|
| **Minor** (soft tissue, whiplash) | $10,000 - $25,000 | Short recovery, no surgery |
| **Moderate** (herniated disc, fractures) | $25,000 - $100,000 | Requires PT, injections |
| **Severe** (surgery required) | $100,000 - $250,000 | Spinal fusion, long-term care |
| **Catastrophic** (paralysis, TBI) | $250,000 - $1,000,000+ | Life-altering injuries |

- **National Average:** $37,000 - $55,000
- **Median:** $20,000 - $40,000

## Important Disclaimers

1. **These are estimates only.** Actual settlement values depend on specific case factors including injury severity, liability clarity, jurisdiction, insurance policy limits, and available evidence.

2. **State variations exist.** Some states have damage caps, different liability standards, or no-fault insurance systems that significantly impact settlement values.

3. **Medical expenses matter.** Settlement amounts often correlate with documented medical treatment costs.

4. **Time affects value.** Settlements may vary based on how long ago the injury occurred and statute of limitations considerations.
