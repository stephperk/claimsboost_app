Implementation Notes

Option 1 (Trust Badges + Statistics): Clean and professional, great for establishing credibility with concrete numbers.

Option 3 (As Seen On): Minimalist approach, works well if you have actual partner logos or media mentions.

Option 4 (Value Props): Blue gradient maintains color consistency while highlighting key benefits.

Option 5 (Testimonials): Strongest social proof through real client stories and ratings.

Option 6 (Orange Trust Banner): Most brand-forward option using primary orange color with reassuring messaging.
