Design Recommendation

Recommended: Option 2 - Two-Row Stacked Footer

Why this option works best for ClaimsBoost:

  • Perfect balance of simplicity and professionalism - The two-tier structure feels polished and organized without being overly complex
  • Excellent scalability - If you need to add more links or sections in the future, this layout can easily accommodate them without a complete redesign
  • Clear visual hierarchy - The top row emphasizes brand identity and navigation, while the bottom row handles legal requirements and copyright
  • Responsive-friendly - Works beautifully across all screen sizes with minimal adjustments needed
  • Industry-appropriate - For a legal services platform, this strikes the right balance between modern minimalism and professional credibility
  • Maintains brand presence - The logo gets prominent placement without overwhelming the other elements

Implementation Notes:

  • The dark background (#1a1a1a) and orange hover color (#FF7B00) maintain brand consistency
  • Social icons now include LinkedIn alongside Facebook and the updated X logo
  • All removed sections (Careers, Press, For Users, For Lawyers) are eliminated as requested
  • Spacing and typography match the existing footer exactly
  • The design degrades gracefully on mobile devices

Alternative Choice: Option 3 - Centered Minimal

If you want a more modern, brand-focused aesthetic and don't anticipate adding more content, Option 3 offers a sophisticated, elegant alternative. The centered layout puts maximum emphasis on the ClaimsBoost brand and creates a memorable, refined impression.
