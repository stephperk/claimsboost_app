Stats Banner V2 - Inline Variations

Six variations on the inline sentence style (Option 3), exploring different layouts, backgrounds, and number treatments.

Option 1: Extra Large Numbers

Same inline flow but with significantly larger numbers for maximum impact.

Search 5,000 law firms representing over $5B+ recovered for victims

Pros

  • Numbers create strong visual anchor
  • Maintains readable sentence flow
  • Clear hierarchy between numbers and text
  • Very premium and confident feel

Cons

  • Large size difference may feel unbalanced
  • Requires more vertical space for line height

Option 2: Baseline Aligned

Numbers and labels aligned on baseline with lighter connecting text.

Search 5,000 law firms representing over $5B+ recovered for victims

Pros

  • Clear pairing of numbers with their labels
  • Connecting text is de-emphasized elegantly
  • Flexible wrapping on smaller screens
  • Professional editorial feel

Cons

  • Multiple font weights may feel busy
  • Wrapping behavior needs careful testing

Option 3: Soft Blue Background

Subtle blue gradient background adds depth and visual separation.

Search 5,000 law firms representing over $5B+ recovered for victims

Pros

  • Background adds visual interest without distraction
  • Blue tones reinforce brand identity
  • Creates clear section break from hero
  • Feels trustworthy and professional

Cons

  • Subtle effect may go unnoticed
  • Could clash with hero if not carefully positioned

Option 4: Two-Line Split

Intro text on first line, stats prominently displayed on second line.

Search our network of

5,000 law firms
representing
$5B+ recovered for victims

Pros

  • Stats are the clear focal point
  • Very scannable - numbers jump out
  • Labels directly paired with numbers
  • Clean mobile adaptation

Cons

  • Loses the natural sentence flow
  • Takes more vertical space

Option 5: Dark Mode Inline

Same inline sentence on dark background for contrast and premium feel.

Search 5,000 law firms representing over $5B+ recovered for victims

Pros

  • Strong visual break from white hero
  • Numbers glow beautifully on dark
  • Very premium and authoritative
  • Maintains readable sentence flow

Cons

  • Dark band may feel heavy
  • Could disrupt page flow

Option 6: Underlined Numbers

Compact layout with subtle gradient underlines on numbers for accent.

Search 5,000 law firms representing over $5B+ recovered for victims

Pros

  • Underlines add visual interest subtly
  • Most compact vertical footprint
  • Solid color numbers with gradient accent
  • Elegant and refined appearance

Cons

  • Underlines may be too subtle
  • Less dramatic impact than larger options

Recommendation

Option 1 (Extra Large Numbers) delivers the most premium feel while preserving the natural sentence flow you liked. The 56px numbers create immediate visual impact while the surrounding text remains readable.

Alternative: If you want more visual separation from the hero, Option 3 (Soft Blue Background) adds depth while staying light and approachable. For maximum scannability, Option 4 (Two-Line Split) makes the numbers impossible to miss.
