Recommendation

Option 1 (Minimal Dark Bar) offers the best balance of premium feel and usability. The dark background creates a clear visual break from the white hero section, while the blue gradient numbers maintain brand consistency. It's impactful without being overwhelming, and the horizontal layout works well on all screen sizes.

Alternative: If you want a warmer, more approachable feel, Option 5 (Floating Card) is excellent. For maximum premium impact, consider Option 4 (Premium Gold) which ties into your orange CTA buttons while feeling luxurious.
