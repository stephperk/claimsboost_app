Recommendation: Option 2 or Option 3

Option 2 (Left-Aligned Vertical) provides the best balance if you want consistency with your existing settlement cards. The left-aligned category label at the top matches the settlement card pattern exactly, and the vertical flow is familiar to users.

Option 3 (Horizontal Layout) is recommended if you want better space efficiency and clearer visual distinction from settlement cards. The horizontal layout with icon on the left creates a nice visual anchor and allows for more compact cards that still display all information clearly.

Both options maintain the exact category label styling from your settlement cards while providing clear information hierarchy and good responsive behavior.
